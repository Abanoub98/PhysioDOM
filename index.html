Our Benefits

Spinal Manupulation

A technique where practitioners use their hands or a device to apply a controlled thrust to a joint of your spine.

Medical Acupuncture

Medical Acupuncture coverage includes: Pain relief, Digestive disorders, Neurological disorders, Respiratory disorders, Dermatological conditions.

Electrotherapy

Electrotherapy is the use of electrical energy as a medical treatment, use of electric current to speed wound healing.

Therapeutic Exercise

They are movements and physical activities designed to restore function and flexibility, improve strength and decrease pain.

Manual Lympahtic

MLD is to augment contractions and stimulate the lymph nodes to improve their activity to reroute stagnant lymphatic fluid.

Joint Mobilization

is a manual therapy intervention, a type of straight-lined, passive movement of a skeletal joint that addresses arthrokinematic joint motion (joint gliding) rather than osteokinematic joint motion.
